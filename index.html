Features

Efficient Navigation

By using crowdsource data and Smartcar's API, Traffeco provides you with efficient routing navigation. Drivers will have access to optimal speed data to streamline travel.

Connect with Friends

Connect with friends and family to share eco-scores and inspire one another to drive more eco friendly. Traffeco calculates eco-scores based on your driving habits that help the environment.

Earn Rewards

Be rewarded for safe and efficient driving. By following Traffeco's driving tips, you will be building eco friendly driving habits and earning rewards based on your eco-scores.

About us

A Greener Planet with Less Traffic

Our team envisions a future of optimal travel. With a solution-focused and human-centered mindset, we wanted to not only create a healthier planet but also make travel more efficient and fun for everyone. We use the Smartcar API and crowdsource data to provide you with effective routing navigation that inspires eco friendly actions.
